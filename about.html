What is Time Planner?

The Time Planner System is a digital tool designed to help students and busy individuals manage their daily activities more effectively. It combines task management with mental health support by providing:

  • Add, update, and delete tasks
  • Set reminders for deadlines
  • Track your daily moods
  • Write and reflect through a personal journal

Our Mission

To create a simple, user-friendly system that improves productivity while supporting mental wellness. This project was built with students in mind, but it can also benefit professionals and anyone who wants to stay organized.
